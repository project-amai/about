Regarding amai streaming end of development

28-01-2023

Hello everyone!
I just wanted to let you guys know that amai streaming will reach its end of development state starting 1st February, 2023. However, the site will still be available for use as usual. But don't worry, there are plenty of other great options out there for streaming anime for free. We will continue to work on new and exciting projects, and we appreciate your support along the way!

Thank you! :)
